About Rock And Rise

The Rock N Rise was created with a vision of bringing all the music lovers together and promote local music. Founded by the youths of Bongaigaon and Chirang. The Rock N Rise organization aim to provide a better platform to the young musicians seeking higher opportunities in the field of music.

MISSION
“ To support and motivate the young and talented musicians ”

The members of The Rock N Rise organization seek to bring all the music lovers together and their goal is to continuously spread the message of love and passion towards music. This point is driven by conversations that may very well begin with an event like Rock N Rise where the versatility brings unity.
